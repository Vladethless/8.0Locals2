Wood shake siding

HomeWood shake siding

Wood has been a luxury material for housing and a symbol of class and good taste for centuries. It timelessly maintains its reputation and is the dream material for many.

The house siding is arguably the most dominant and extensive part of your exterior that frames your entire home. It is imperative to not only the aesthetics and overall look of your house but also your house protection and market value. Why not opt for wood as the siding material and make a classy statement with your house.

This natural and organic material isn't only about looks, though; it is on par with other siding materials when it comes to functionality as well. Get the best of both worlds with wood shakes siding!

This siding satisfies all the requirements you might have for a siding material which include:

Variety

The good thing about using wood as a material is that it can be painted in any color of your preference to match your exterior. Not only that, but the wood shingles can also be fashioned in any style of your choice, giving you all the diversity you need in options.

You can also choose from various stains and textures for the wood used in your siding. You can fashion the bevel, board and batten, tongue and groove, channel, and wavy edge level. Furthermore, you can develop attractive exterior options for your house, giving it the dream makeover you want.

Durable

You can also expect your wood shake siding to last in the long run as it is a durable material. Also, any fear of moisture damage and pest infestations can quickly be expelled by treating the wood so that it resists them.

It can also reflect the sun's UV rays and help keep your house cool on hot days. This is because the wood used when making wood shake siding is usually less dense and does not allow heat transfer easily. This can help you save on your overall energy costs.

Better House Resale Value

Since it is high quality and expensive material, using it for your siding will attract more interested buyers (if you're planning on selling) and increase the prices they are willing to offer for the house. This luxury material will significantly boost your house's value, and potential buyers will appreciate the house's design and style.

Environmentally Friendly

Another good thing about wood shake siding is that it is environmentally friendly. In addition to wood being a renewable material, its utilization also does not harm the environment and does not leave a negative environmental footprint. It is also biodegradable and will quickly decompose once discarded. Hence it is a sustainable material choice.
